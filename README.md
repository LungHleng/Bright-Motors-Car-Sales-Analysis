# Bright-Motors-Car-Sales-Analysis

Project Overview: 
This project analyzes historical car sales data from of Bright Motors to provide actionable insights for the newly appointed Head of Sales. The goal is to identify revenue drivers, regional performance, customer preferences, and profitability trends to guide future sales and marketing strategies.

Objectives: 
- Identify top-performing makes and models by revenue.  
- Explore the relationship between price, mileage, and year of manufacture.  
- Analyze regional sales performance across states.  
- Detect emerging customer purchasing trends (fuel type, body style, etc.).  
- Recommend strategies to increase dealership profitability and efficiency.  

Tools & Technologies:
- Data Processing: Databricks   
- Visualization: Excel, Google Looker Studio  
- Presentation: PowerPoint, Lovable
- Planning: Miro, Gantt Chart
